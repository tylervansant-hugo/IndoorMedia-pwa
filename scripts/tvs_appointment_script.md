# TVS Appointment Setting Script

_Cold walk-in / appointment-setting script for IndoorMedia reps._

---

Hey there,

I was hoping you could point me in the right direction… My name is **____** and I am working with the **_____** store down the road. Reason for the visit is we're kicking off a big promotion over at the store and will be featuring and recommending just a few local businesses to all their customers.

We see huge success for other businesses like yours and some of your neighbors as well _(give examples if you have them)_.

**Who should I talk to about doing the same for your business?**

---

### (If it's the person you're talking to)

Oh perfect, well what I'd like to do is schedule a brief meeting to learn more about your business, share what we do for similar local businesses, and most importantly what we could do for you.

I can be back in about **15 minutes** or at **____**. Which works best for you?

Great! And I'm sure you're going to love it. If for some reason you don't, you have no problem just telling me no, right?

But on the other hand, when you love it like I predict — **is there anyone else who needs to see my program in order to say yes?**

---

_⚠️ Make sure that you schedule for a time that fits all parties (including any other decision-makers)._

---

### Coaching Notes

- **Pattern interrupt + social proof** opens the door without a hard pitch.
- **"Be back in 15 minutes or at ____"** is an assumptive close — keep it confident.
- **"You have no problem telling me no, right?"** lowers resistance (tie-down).
- **The decision-maker question is the money line.** Don't skip it — it prevents wasted closes where the buyer says "I need to check with my partner."
