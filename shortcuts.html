📱 Setup Instructions

  1. Open the Shortcuts app on your iPhone
  2. Tap + to create a new shortcut
  3. For "Sales Briefing": Add action → Open URL → paste: https://tylervansant-hugo.github.io/IndoorMedia-pwa/?mode=driving
  4. Tap the name at top → rename to "imPro Briefing"
  5. Tap ℹ️ → Add to Siri → record phrase "Sales briefing"
  6. Repeat for other shortcuts:
    • "Next appointment": URL = https://tylervansant-hugo.github.io/IndoorMedia-pwa/?mode=driving
    • "Log a call": URL = https://tylervansant-hugo.github.io/IndoorMedia-pwa/?action=logcall
  7. These shortcuts work through CarPlay automatically — just say the phrase!
