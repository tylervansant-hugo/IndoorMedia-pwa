# Register Tape Sales Cheat Sheet

## THE PRESENTATION (In-Person, Every Time)

### 1. WARM UP (2 min)
- Ask about their business (how long, what's working, community involvement)
- Get them talking — make notes

### 2. THE HOOK (1 min)
**"Do people really use these things?"**
- Option A (relates to busy owner): "I'm the biggest hypocrite. Only use coupons when my spouse tells me to."
- Option B (relates to decision-maker): "I look for ways to save money eating out. Who's the real decision-maker in the house? WHO'S SHOPPING?"
- **Key point**: Most customers come from 1-3 mile radius. We target the decision-makers who SHOP.

### 3. THE NUMBERS (3 min)
- Show: 25,000 people per week walk through [competitor chain]
- Calculate with calculator: 25 cases × 50 rolls × 137 segments × repeats = **4,327 impressions per day**
- **BE ANIMATED. This is the wow.**
- Compare to their flyers: They get thrown away. Ours get seen repeatedly (days 2, 4, 7, 9, 13...) — all month long.

### 4. THE PROOF (2 min)
- Show video testimonials (your category)
- Show local success: "600 PER MONTH!!!"
- Written testimonials with NUMBERS
- Ask: "I'm sure you can handle another 10-20 customers per day?"

### 5. LOCATION SELECTION (1 min)
- Show map of closest stores
- "Which would you prefer to promote you?"

### 6. THE OFFER (2 min)
- Programs: 12, 24, or 36 months
- We supply 90 days of tape — you change ad every quarter
- Cost: $[Z] per month (or 3 installments or pay-in-full discount)
- **"What kind of offers do you think you might want to do?"** (Let them start talking about offers)

### 7. IF THEY HESITATE → SIMPLE PATH CLOSE
- Acknowledge: "I absolutely understand..."
- Fear of loss: "Unfortunately my situation being what it is, I am done out here."
- Hypothetical: "...if I printed 3,976 messages and handed them out across the street..."
- Alternative: "What if I could save you a ton of money? Would that give me a chance to earn your business today?"
- **Push for binary YES/NO. Not maybe.**

---

## WEEKLY GOALS (Non-Negotiable)

✅ **5+ doors knocked or calls made per day** (25+/week)
✅ **At least 2 in-person presentations** (following all 7 steps)
✅ **1 close attempt minimum** (using Simple Path if needed)
✅ **Target: 1 close per week** ($4,000 avg)

---

## REMEMBER
- **You are handing people a coupon they WANT**
- **Fear of loss beats fear of commitment**
- **Every "maybe" should get the close**
- **No presentation? No sale. Ever.**
- **Local social proof wins — show testimonials**
